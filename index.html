PROJECTS

Shotgo

Shogo is a multiplayer pogo mayhem first person shooter, play online with others and rise to the top of the score board. Controlled movement is key to winning the game as your momentum can give you an edge over your opponents. find all kinds of cool weapons troughout the level and spread the mayhem.
Combining tetris and racing!

Tetracer

Tetracer is a challenging but fun arcade racing game where you need to driver over temporary placed platforms that are being build by your buddy or the AI. The challenge is to move as quickly as possible while not falling of the platforms or hitting an obstacle. This game is meant to enable fun and to challenge you to beat the highscore.
A underwater exploration game created in C++

The Deep Blue

The Deep Blue is a game we've created with a group of 6 people within 4 weeks as a school assignment. The game was created 'from scratch' using our self-written engine in C++. Most of the development on the engine was also done within those 4 weeks.

In the game you're diving, finding signs of an ancient civilization which lived on the bottom of the ocean. Your goal: Figure out what happened to this civilization.
Combining games and business.

TeamNow

Are your people aware of the progress they’re making? Do they know how other teams are performing? With our gamification platform your people will go on a journey in a fun way while they're making their team stronger and stronger.

TeamNow is a gamification project created for a company called TeamValue, based in Zwolle, The Netherlands. The goal from this project is to give people within a company, insight as to how teams within the company are performing. By visualizing it in a fun way, it attracts the attention, and keeps people interested.
A first person tower defense game

Droid Defense

Droid Defense is a game we've created with a group of 5 people within 3 weeks as a school assignment. We used Unity as game engine for our game. In the game your goal is to protect your base, you can do so by placing turrets, walls, etc., which will shoot incoming waves of enemies. Next to placing turrets, you can also run around yourself and shoot the enemies attacking.
